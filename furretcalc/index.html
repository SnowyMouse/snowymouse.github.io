How do I fill this out?

Please enter your stats as displayed in the status screen, entering your Pokémon's current HP.

NOTE: This tool is NOT a stat calculator. It is not assumed that you precisely know what your Pokémon's stat experience and IVs (DVs) are at a given fight, nor is it needed for damage calcs. A stat calculator will be added later, however, to assist with planning.

IVs (DVs) are only necessary for calculating Hidden Power. You can leave them at 0, otherwise.

Friendship is only necessary for Return and Frustration. You can leave it at 0, otherwise. Note than NPC trainers will have 0 friendship on unmodified games.

Badges are needed to calculate badge boosts. These only apply in normal in-game battles, however.
