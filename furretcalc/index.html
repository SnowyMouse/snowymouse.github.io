How do I fill this out?

First, you will want to enter your stats.

  • Automatic stat input: Please enter your IVs (DVs) and stat experience. Your stats will be automatically calculated.
  • Manual stat input: Please enter your stats as displayed in the status screen. This is handy for playthroughs where you only know the stats in the stat screen. You can still opt to input IVs (DVs) for calculating Hidden Power.

Friendship is only necessary for Return and Frustration. You can leave it at 0, otherwise. Note than NPC trainers will have 0 friendship on unmodified games.

Badges are needed to calculate badge boosts. These only apply in normal in-game battles, however.
