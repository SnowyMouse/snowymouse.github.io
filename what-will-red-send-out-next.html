The order in which Pokémon are sent out in Gen 2 follows a very simple pattern:

  1. Give every Pokémon on the AI's team a score of 0.
  2. For every Pokémon that is weak to your Pokémon's primary or secondary type, subtract 1.
  3. For every Pokémon that has a move with a supereffective type (Psybeam and Amnesia are treated the same), add 1.
  4. Go in team order and find the first Pokémon that has +1.
  5. No +1? Go in team order and find the first Pokémon that has +0.
  6. No +0? Choose a random Pokémon.
